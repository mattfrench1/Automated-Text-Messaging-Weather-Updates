About the Weather Features

OpenWeather API

The weather data will be generated from the OpenWeather API. This ensures the highest accuracy possible to provide users with reliable weather updates.

Forecast

Forecast for the day will be provided so users can receive weather updates before they leave to determine what clothes they should wear for the day.

Pollen

Pollen levels in certain plants/trees that individuals may be sensitive to will be provided.

Solar Levels

Solar levels will provide users with data regarding how strong the Sun will be to give them an idea if they should wear Sun protective clothing or sunscreen.
